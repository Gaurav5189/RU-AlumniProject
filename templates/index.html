    OUR ILLUSTRIOUS ALUMNI

    BIJU PATNAIK
    1932

    Bijayananda Patnaik, widely known as Biju Patnaik, was a distinguished alumnus of Ravenshaw College, where he enrolled in the intermediate science program in 1932. During his time at Ravenshaw, he excelled as a sportsman, leading the college teams in football, hockey, athletics, and cricket, and was recognized as the sports champion for three consecutive years. His exposure to Mahatma Gandhi during a Khadi Tour to Odisha profoundly influenced his commitment to India's independence movement. Driven by a passion for aviation, he left Ravenshaw to pursue aeronautical training in Delhi, eventually becoming a skilled pilot. His daring missions during World War II and the Indian freedom struggle earned him national acclaim. Biju Patnaik's legacy as a freedom fighter, aviator, and statesman continues to inspire, reflecting the values and spirit nurtured during his formative years at Ravenshaw.

    DR. DIPTI RANJAN MOHARANA
    1999-2001

    Distinguished alumnus of Ravenshaw College,completed his Master of Arts in English from Ravenshaw. He currently serves as the Director of Students' Welfare (DSW) at Ravenshaw University, where he oversees various student welfare activities and acts as a bridge between students and the administration. Dr. Moharana is deeply committed to encouraging student-led initiatives that enrich campus life and foster a supportive, inclusive environment. His leadership focuses on the holistic development of students, nurturing them academically, personally, and professionally. Under his guidance, several impactful programs have been launched to promote student growth and engagement. His dedication and passion for education embody the values of Ravenshaw, making him a role model for the entire Ravenshaw community. From a proud student to a respected leader, Dr. Moharana's journey continues to inspire generations.

    MR. ANANDA KUMAR
    2015-2018

    Ananda Kumar, an esteemed alumnus of Ravenshaw University, graduated in 2018 with a degree in Computer Science from the university's renowned department. Since then, he has built a successful career at Amazon, specializing in brand management. With his strong technical background and expertise in branding, Ananda has been instrumental in driving key strategies that enhance brand visibility and consumer engagement. His innovative approach and attention to detail have earned him recognition in the field. As a Ravenshaw graduate, Ananda's journey reflects the impact of a solid educational foundation combined with hands-on experience in the global tech industry. His story serves as an inspiration to current students, demonstrating how passion and dedication can lead to success in highly competitive fields.

    Mr. Dipak Ranjan Das
    1989

    Distinguished alumnus of Ravenshaw College, graduated in the Class of 1989. With a strong academic foundation built at Ravenshaw, he pursued a career in medicine and has made significant contributions to the field of cardiology. Currently, he serves as an Associate Professor of Cardiology at SCB Medical College and Hospital, one of India's premier medical institutions. In his role, Mr. Das combines clinical expertise with academic leadership, mentoring future medical professionals and advancing cardiac healthcare. His journey from a Ravenshaw graduate to a respected government officer and educator stands as a testament to his dedication, excellence, and commitment to public service. Mr. Das continues to inspire both the medical community and the Ravenshaw fraternity through his remarkable achievements and leadership.
