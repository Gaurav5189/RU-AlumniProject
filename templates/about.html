Our Legacy

It's important to stay connected to your roots.

Established in 1868 as one of the oldest institutions in the state of Odisha, Ravenshaw University has nurtured generations of students who have gone on to make significant contributions in various fields. Our alumni community is a testament to the vibrant legacy of Ravenshaw, uniting graduates worldwide and inspiring them to remain connected to their alumnus.

"To foster a community of lifelong learners and innovators who contribute to society through world-class academics, research, and service."

History & Legacy

Founded in 1868, Ravenshaw has a rich history spanning over 150 years. What began as a provincial college has evolved into a premier university, nurturing generations of leaders, thinkers, and innovators who have made significant contributions across various fields.
